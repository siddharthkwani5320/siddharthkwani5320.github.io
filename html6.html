HTML Links

A hyperlink (or link) is a word, group of words, or image that you can click on to jump to another document. When you move the cursor over a link in a Web page, the arrow will turn into a little hand. The most important attribute of the element is the href attribute, which indicates the links destination. By default, links will appear as follows in all browsers

  • An unvisited link is underlined and blue
  • A visited link is underlined and purple
  • An active link is underlined and red
